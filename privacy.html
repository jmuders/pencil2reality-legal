This Privacy Policy explains how Pencil2Reality ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the Pencil2Reality mobile application and related services (the "Service"). Pencil2Reality allows users to transform children's drawings and other sketches into AI-generated images in styles such as photorealistic, cartoon, fantasy, and comic book.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account Information: such as your email address or sign-in details when you create an account.
  • User Content: including drawings, uploaded images, prompts, and generated images.
  • Usage Data: such as app interactions, features used, session activity, and crash diagnostics.
  • Device and Log Information: such as device model, operating system, app version, IP address, and access times.
  • Purchase Information: information related to subscriptions, credits, or in-app purchases, as provided by the relevant app store or payment processor. We do not store full payment card details ourselves.

2. How We Use Information

We use information we collect to:

  • provide, operate, and maintain the Service;
  • create and manage user accounts;
  • process drawings, prompts, and images to generate AI-based outputs;
  • deliver subscriptions, credits, and other app features;
  • improve product performance, reliability, and user experience;
  • respond to support requests and user communications;
  • monitor misuse, prevent fraud, and protect the security of the Service; and
  • comply with applicable legal obligations.

3. AI and Third-Party Processing

To provide image-generation features, user content such as drawings, prompts, and related inputs may be transmitted to third-party service providers, including OpenAI, for processing. We also use Supabase for backend infrastructure, authentication, database, and storage services.

These providers may process data on our behalf in accordance with their own terms and privacy policies. We do not use your content to identify you personally beyond what is needed to provide the Service, secure the platform, and comply with law.

4. Legal Bases for Processing

Where required by applicable law, we process personal information on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • performance of a contract with you;
  • your consent;
  • our legitimate interests in operating, securing, and improving the Service; and
  • compliance with legal obligations.

5. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information:

  • with service providers that help us operate the app, including hosting, storage, analytics, AI processing, and customer support providers;
  • with app stores or payment partners to manage subscriptions and purchases;
  • when required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request;
  • to protect rights, safety, security, and prevent fraud or abuse; and
  • in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of all or part of our business.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide the Service, maintain your account, resolve disputes, enforce our agreements, and comply with legal obligations. Generated images and uploaded content may remain in your account until you delete them, delete your account, or request deletion, subject to legal or operational retention requirements.

7. Children's Privacy

Pencil2Reality may be used to transform children's drawings, but the Service is intended to be used by a parent, guardian, or other authorized adult, or under adult supervision where required by law. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children in violation of applicable law.

If you believe a child has provided personal information to us in a way that violates applicable law, please contact us so we can investigate and take appropriate action.

To exercise these rights, contact us at jmuders@globalcitizensolutions.com.
